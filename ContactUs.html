Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the Enterprise Fleet package?
Our Enterprise Fleet package includes priority scheduling, dedicated account management, bi-weekly deep cleaning, and monthly ceramic coat maintenance for fleets of 10 or more vehicles.
How long does a premium ceramic coating take?
A standard premium ceramic coating application requires 24 to 48 hours. This includes thorough paint correction, application, and the necessary curing time in our climate-controlled facility.
Do you offer mobile detailing services?
Yes, we offer premium mobile detailing for individual owners and smaller fleets within a 25-mile radius of our HQ. A mobile service fee may apply.
